# Prompts Used

## Prompt 1

Act as a professional social media marketer.

Create UGC-style advertisements for a skincare product called "GlowSkin Face Wash".

Include:

- 5 attention-grabbing hooks
- 3 Instagram captions
- 3 Call-to-Action statements
- 2 short video ad scripts

Tone:
Friendly, natural, and persuasive.

Target Audience:
College students and young professionals.
